Pizza

Description

Pizza is a classic Italian dish that is enjoyed all over the world. It consists of a flat bread base topped with tomato sauce,cheese, and a variety of vegetables or other toppings. Pizza is popular because it is filling, tasty, and easy to customize based on personal preference.

This homemade vegetable pizza is simple to prepare and uses basic ingredients that are easily available. By adding fresh vegetables and controlling the amount of cheese and oil, it can also be made healthier. It is perfect for a quick meal, family dinner, or casual get-together.

Ingredients

  • Pizza base
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarrella Cheese
  • Onion
  • Spinach
  • Basil
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Oregano and chilli flakes

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C.
  2. Place the pizza base on a tray.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly on the base.
  4. Add vegetables on top.
  5. Sprinkle grated mozarrella cheese.
  6. Add salt, oregano, and chili flakes.
  7. Drizzle a little olive oil.
  8. Bake for 10–15 minutes until cheese melts.
