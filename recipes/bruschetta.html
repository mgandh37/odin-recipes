Bruschetta

Description

Bruschetta is a simple and refreshing Italian starter that is quick to prepare. The combination of fresh tomatoes, garlic, and olive oil gives it a light and flavorful taste.

This dish is best served fresh and is perfect as a snack, starter, or party appetizer. It uses basic ingredients and does not require cooking apart from toasting the bread.

Ingredients

  • Bread slices (baguette or any crusty bread)
  • Tomatoes (finely chopped)
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Fresh basil or mixed herbs(optional)

Steps

  1. Toast the bread slices until lightly crisp.
  2. In a bowl, mix chopped tomatoes, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  3. Add olive oil and mix well.
  4. Spoon the tomato mixture onto the toasted bread.
  5. Garnish with basil or herbs.
  6. Serve immediately.
