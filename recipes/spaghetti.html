Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti is a simple yet delicious pasta dish that can be made quickly with basic ingredients.

The tomato-based sauce gives it a rich flavor, while garlic and herbs add aroma and taste.

This recipe is easy to customize by adding vegetables like mushrooms, bell peppers, or olives. It is a great option for a quick homemade meal and is enjoyed by people of all ages.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Onion (finely chopped)
  • Garlic (chopped)
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Mixed herbs or oregano
  • Chili flakes (optional)
  • Grated parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil water in a large pot and add salt.
  2. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions and drain.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan.
  4. Add garlic and onion and saut√© until soft.
  5. Add tomato sauce, salt, pepper, and herbs.
  6. Cook the sauce for a few minutes.
  7. Add the cooked spaghetti to the sauce and mix well.
  8. Serve hot with grated cheese if desired.
